[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I pour a fresh coffee and wait by the phone
This quiet room echoes when I am alone
Just craving the sound of your midnight sigh
I need your sweet whisper to get me by

[Verse 1]
The fire is burning and casting a glow
I keep walking the floor and I am pacing it slow
I remember the taste of the rain on your skin
And the thrill in my chest when you invited me in

[Pre-Chorus]
Every tick of the clock is a beat of my heart
I hate every second we spend far apart
I am closing my eyes and I am pulling you near
Just breathing the secrets I want you to hear

[Chorus]
I am missing your voice in the dark of the night
Missing the way that you hold me so tight
A sweet spoken promise that is driving me wild
I am lost in the thought of your beautiful smile

[Post-Chorus]
[Ad-lib] (Calling out to you)
(Oh, I just need to hear you now)
